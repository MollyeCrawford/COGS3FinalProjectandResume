About Me!

My name is Molly Crawford. I am a digital artist and designer from Southern California. I have a strong passion for digital media and love to express myself through my work.

I first learned art traditionally in school and through practice. In 2020, I opened up my own art and jewelry shop where I sold handcrafted goods this allowed me to grow my artistic abilities greatly and set myself up for a long career in the art field. I learned a lot about my aesthetic preferences and learned to develop a style that is uniquely.... me!
